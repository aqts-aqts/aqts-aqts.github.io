The Enigma

In WWII, the Germans used secret encryption devices to encode messages so they could not be understood if intercepted. Can you create your own version of the Enigma?

Requirements:

  1. Ability to encode numbers and letters.
  2. Decode encoded messages with 100% accuracy.
  3. Have fun!

Challenge: Can you do this without using any random modules?
